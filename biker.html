MX Gear - Moto Gear Sets for Men

Fox Racing's MX gear sets are the cornerstone of what we do. The new line of FOX gear, featuring the technically advanced FlexAir collection, is the highest performance race wear on the market today. Developed by Fox team riders like Ryan Dungey and Ken Roczen, it features our proprietary Fox TruDri moisture transfer performance fabric technology. 25% lighter than standard jersey material, the Fox FlexAir gear sets offer unrestricted freedom of movement due to the innovative, 4-way stretch, TruMotion chassis.
